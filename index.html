Your Safety, Our Priority

HERGuardian: SafeSheRide is an AI-powered women's safety app that provides real-time security alerts, verified women-only ride services, and secure FinTech integration for Africa's digital future.

Powerful Features

HERGuardian combines cutting-edge technology with user-centric design to deliver unparalleled safety solutions

AI-Powered Emergency SOS

One-tap panic button that instantly alerts trusted contacts with live location, audio, and video. Works offline-first, sending alerts when signal returns.

Smart Danger Prediction

Machine learning assesses area safety based on historical crime data, user reports, and time of day. Recommends safer routes in real time.

SafeSheRide

Women-only ride service with pre-verified drivers. In-app ride tracking, driver profiles, and secure anonymous chat.

FinTech Integration

Secure digital wallet for ride payments. Support for SnapScan, MoMo, EFTs, or crypto donations to GBV shelters.

Cybersecurity

Biometric login and OTP verification. End-to-end encryption for all communications. POPIA-compliant data protection.

Support & Resources

One-tap access to hotlines, legal aid, and mental health services. Directory of trusted local NGOs and shelters.

How It Works

Simple steps to empower your safety journey

1

Download & Setup

Install HERGuardian from your app store, create your profile, and set up your emergency contacts. The process takes less than 5 minutes.

2

Configure Your Safety

Customize your safety preferences - set up regular check-ins, define safe zones, and choose your notification preferences.

3

Stay Protected

Go about your day with confidence. HERGuardian works silently in the background, ready to alert your network at the first sign of trouble.

4

Access Support When Needed

If you ever feel unsafe or need assistance, one tap connects you to emergency services, trusted contacts, or our women-only ride network.
